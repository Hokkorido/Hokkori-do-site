About the Therapist

Yu Liquorman began her massage journey in 2004 in Japan, where she trained in aromatherapy massage and worked at the well-regarded salon Mont St. Michele. After relocating to the United States in 2008, she earned her California Massage Therapy Council certification and continued her career at Spa Rilaken in Torrance, CA. In 2012, she received her Reiki Practitioner certification, expanding her practice to include energy healing. After stepping away from massage work to raise her children, she is now opening her own salon.
